## What to Avoid

1. Choosing purely by the lowest launch price — always calculate the 24-month total.  
2. Ignoring renewal pricing — this is where most “budget” hosts become expensive.  
3. Using shared hosting for sites with real traffic or paid ads.  
4. Skipping staging environments when testing changes.  
5. Treating hosting as “set and forget”.

## Quick Migration Checklist

- Take a full backup with UpdraftPlus  
- Use the new host’s migration tool (Hostinger offers free automated migration)  
- Test on staging first  
- Update DNS only after everything works  
- Verify SSL and Search Console after migration
